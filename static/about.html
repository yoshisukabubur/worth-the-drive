About – Worth The Drive?

Back to app

A lightweight calculator built to solve the classic problem: driving farther for cheaper gas can still cost you more once detour fuel is included.

What this app does

  • Find which candidate is actually best within your time tolerance
  • Show Net savings after subtracting detour fuel cost
  • Compare stations with both map (Google Maps) and ranked list

Data note: Nearby station names and prices in results may be illustrative / demo for now. The math (distance, time, net savings) is real; plugging in live station feeds is a planned upgrade.

What is Net savings?

Net savings = money saved by buying cheaper gas compared to baseline minus extra fuel cost caused by the detour drive.

MPG and speed are estimates. You can adjust them in Advanced settings.

Privacy

  • Location is obtained on your device. It is used for calculation but not stored permanently on the server.
  • A simple free-tier usage limit (IP-based) is used to keep service costs under control.
